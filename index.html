🎙️ Agentic AI · Takeda R&D

AI-Powered PDF-to-Audio QA System

Agentic system that converts any PDF into a voice-queryable knowledge base. Users upload a clinical or regulatory document, ask questions via audio, and receive spoken answers — bridging the gap between static PDFs and audio-first workflows. Uses SentenceTransformer embeddings for semantic retrieval, PyMuPDF for extraction, SpeechRecognition for STT, and gTTS for audio output via a Gradio interface. Proposed use cases include clinical trial summarization, pharmacovigilance safety retrieval, regulatory compliance (IND/NDA/CTD), and investigator brochure querying.

Patient Discontinuation Risk Predictor

Deployed ML scoring model that predicts patient-level risk of discontinuing specialty therapy due to high out-of-pocket cost and payer access friction (PA denials, not-covered, SP rejects). Outputs a risk score, high-risk flag, and reason codes (HIGH_OOP_COST, PA_DENIED, NOT_COVERED, PA_PENDING, SP_REJECT_SIGNAL) to enable Patient Services teams to prioritize interventions. Containerized deployment with parameter validation, archived outputs, and a full monitoring plan (data quality, drift detection, lift tracking). Roadmap: weekly cadence scoring → agentic triage workflows → XGBoost trained model → enterprise multi-franchise decisioning.
